Freelance Web Development Done With Intention

Hi, welcome to Vyathan Design. My name is Matt Brundage and I am an independant, freelance, Web Developer located in Central Michigan. I specialize in building websites for clients that value long-term synergetic relationships. I build new websites for people, businesses, or brands, and I revise exsisting websites too. In addition, I also provide technical SEO (Search Engine Optimization) audits, website maintenance, and website hosting.

The websites I build use the latest and most reliable front-end languages like HTML5 and CSS3. I am also skilled with JavaScript and frameworks like React and Vue, allowing me to add interactive functionality to your site. Because I freelance I am able to keep my overhead low and pass the savings on to you in the form of some of the most reasonable prices in the industry.

Having attended Montcalm Community College's Associate of Science - Web Developer & Programming program, as well as preparing to attend Central Michigan University's Bachelor of Science - Computer Science program, I have learned the practical application of Web Development skills needed to complete even your most challenging projects.
